OUR SERVICES

Deeds For All Your Needs

Structural
Design

Team EDEN understands the responsibility towards society in case of structural stability at the same time towards the environment in the matter of consumption of material. That’s why we put the same amount of energy and effort into any scale project to provide the most economical and efficient STRUCTURAL & CIVIL ENGINEERING SOLUTIONS for your project.

Project Management
Consultancy

TIMELY completion of the project and QUALITY assurance gives utility & financial comfort to both end-user and client. EDEN works for that confidence by providing initial budgeting, project scheduling, regular reporting, supervising all site activities, and coordinating the whole project.

Industrial
Architecture

Efficient space planning in terms of functionality and utility arrangements is the first step toward the project. EDEN is well-aware of different industrial segment planning requirements and other utility management to plan the space in the best possible way.

Cost
Consulting

EDEN is well-aware of the costing part of the project. Providing you with the cost estimate, Material quantity surveys, budgeting for various options and also cost-effective solution to utilize the project fund & construction material effectively is our primary concern.

Project
Assistance

EDEN works for you as a helping hand by providing you with trustworthy civil agencies for different civil work, vendors, and suppliers for material purchases, and regular site-visit for quality assurance of various site activities.

Bar Bending
Schedule

This is a value-added service with Structural Design. Effective consumption of steel is one of the most cost-effective solutions in the project. EDEN works to utilize steel more productively by providing Bar-bending & cutting schedule and minimizing wastage.
